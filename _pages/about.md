This is Dr. Onur Kılınççeker's home on the web!

He received his Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Applied Mathematics and Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Information Technologies from Ege University, Turkey. He then earned his Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Electrical Engineering and Information Technologies from Paderborn University, Germany. His doctoral research focused on software testing, specialising in model-based and mutation testing.
